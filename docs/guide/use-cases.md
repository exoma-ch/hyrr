# Use Cases

## Medical isotope production

### Tc-99m via p + Mo-100

The standard medical isotope case: proton bombardment of enriched Mo-100 to produce Tc-99m for SPECT imaging.

- Beam: 16 MeV protons, 0.15 mA
- Target: Enriched Mo-100 (10.22 g/cm³)
- Energy window: 16 → 12 MeV
- Irradiation: 1 day
- Expected Tc-99m activity: ~262 GBq after 1 day cooling
